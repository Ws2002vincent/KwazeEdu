Once upon a time, there was a capybara who lived in the forest. He was a very friendly capybara and he had many friends in the forest. One day, he met a cat who was lost in the forest. The capybara helped the cat find her way home. The cat was very grateful and she became friends with the capybara. They had many adventures together and they lived happily ever after.
